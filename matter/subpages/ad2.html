AD2 - Venturi

Shows the distribution of pressure through a non-uniform cross-section.

Large compressor. Stand with manometer mounted on it. Colour manometer water with KMn04. To fill manometers, place wire down one limb so that air can escape. Ass water to same limb very slowly with tiny beaker or small syringe. Fill to supplied marks and if necessary to remove a little water absorb it in a wad of cotton wiool attached to a thin rod.
