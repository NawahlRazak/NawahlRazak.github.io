HY7 - Archimedes' principle

Demonstrates Archimedes' principle: the buoyant force on an object submerged in a fluid is equal to the weight of the fluid displaced by the object.

Bucket and cylinder experiment. Show that bucket exactly fits cylinder (which can be withdrawn with a gratifying pop. Upthrust due to water displaced by cylinder is exactly balanced by the equal volume of water poured into the bucket.
