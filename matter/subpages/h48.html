H48 - Berthelot Apparatus

For the vaporisation of liquids.

The Berthelot apparatus, originally designed for measuring heat of vaporization, utilizes a closed system to vaporize a liquid and measure the volume of gas displaced. This allows for the determination of the liquid's molar mass or enthalpy of vaporization.
