H2 - Contraction on Cooling

To show force exerted on contraction.

Cast iron bar heated, nut tightened, holding cast iron pin. Bar then cooled by drenching with water from damp cloths. Cast iron pin snaps. Need a photographic dish in which apparatus stands. Two spanners from the workshop, a beaker of cold water and cloths.
