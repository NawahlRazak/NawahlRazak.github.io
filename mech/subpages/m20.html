M20 - Inertial Reference Frame

Demonstrates the concept of an inertial reference frame.

A Perspex box is suspended from a piece of string. One side of the box houses a “gun” aimed at a hole in the other side of the box. The string holding the box is cut and the gun shoots a ball simultaneously. The ball flies directly through the hole.
