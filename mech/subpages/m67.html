M67 - Screwjack

Demonstrates the use of a screwjack which transforms rotational motion into a linear force for lifting loads.

Illustrates the principle that the work done in lifting a large mass a short distance is equivalent to the work done by a small force over a large distance. loads of 1 to 3 kg are suitable for this apparatus.
