M66 - Differential Pulley

Demonstrates the use of a differential pulley

A differential pulley is a mechanical device used for lifting heavy objects with a relatively small amount of force. It consists of two pulleys of different diameters fixed together on a common axle, an endless chain that wraps around them, and a lower pulley block that supports the load. The load is 2kg.
