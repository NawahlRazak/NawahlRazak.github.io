M122 - Torsion & Modulus of Rigidity

Demonstrates the concepts of torsion and the modulus of rigidity.

An iron rod is clamped at one end and twisted at the other by the torque provided by a hanging load. A pointer on the rod indicates rotation. Start with a half-kilogram hanger and add and remove about four 1 kg weights. (Don't try to pass the elastic limit.)
