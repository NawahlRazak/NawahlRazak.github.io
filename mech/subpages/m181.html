M181 - Large Vernier Calipers

Large model of Vernier calipers.

A Vernier scale consists of a ruler and a pair of callipers, one of which is stationary and the other can slide. Slide the callipers so that they grasp an object firmly and use the markings on the ruler and the sliding calliper to obtain a measurement. One or two large models are in existence.
