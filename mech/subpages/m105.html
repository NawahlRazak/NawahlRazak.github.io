M105 - Rotating Candles

Demonstrates the interplay between buoyancy, centripetal force, and the properties of a flame.

The hot gases within the flame are less dense than the surrounding air and are pushed outward by centripetal acceleration. This effect, combined with the natural tendency of the flame to rise due to buoyancy, causes the flame to bend inward towards the center of rotation.
