M184 - Micrometer Screwgauge

A micrometer screw-gauge is a tool used to make fine measurements.

An object is placed between the screw and the calliper. The screw is wound down until it closes firmly on the object. The ruler underneath provides an estimate of the measurement. A cylinder has finer delineations to achieve very accurate measurements. A large model is present to demonstrate this tool.
