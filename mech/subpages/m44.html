M44 - Tangential Motion

Demonstrates Newton's 1st Law.

A marble rolls along the inside of an incomplete metal circle. When the marble reaches a break in the circle, the intuitive expectation is that it will continue moving in a circle. However, in a agreement with Newtonâ€™s 1st law, the ball leaves the circle in the direction of the tangent to the circle at that point (since there are no longer any resultant forces acting on it).
