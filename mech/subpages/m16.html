M11 - Guinea & Feather

Demonstrates the principle of moments.

A metre-stick is suspended at its midpoint from a 2kg spring-balance. The stick can be balanced horizontally by adjusting various weights hung at various distances on either side of the fulcrum. For the stick to be balanced, the sum of clockwise moments and counter-clockwise moments must be equal.
