M103 - Conservation of Momentum: Jet Propulsion

Demonstrates conservation of momentum.

A rotating arm has a socket at one end. A cylinder of compressed CO2 is inserted into the socket. Puncture the cap of the cylinder by means of a special pin. Make sure that the workshop checks the sharpness of the pin before conducting the experiment.
