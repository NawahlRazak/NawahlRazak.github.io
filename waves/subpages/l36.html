L36 - Prism Tower

Shows variation in refractivity.

Vertical “tower” of 60deg prisms of different glasses to show variation in refractivity. Use with adjustable vertical slit. Several spectra are visible at the same time, with different deviations. To see more of them, move the tower up or down on an adjustable stand.
