L76 - Absorption and Reflection

Shows the difference between absorption and reflection colours.

a) Sheet of gold foil between glass plates. Transmits blue-green. b) Interference filters. One transmits mercury-yellow only, appears white by reflected light from the two sides; the other transmits mercury-green only, appears white by reflected light.
